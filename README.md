Project Description
Tammy AI is a voice-based emotional safety companion designed for school environments. Students can express concerns naturally through conversation, and the system analyzes emotional urgency levels to assist counselors in identifying potential risks early.

The Problem statement
Many students hesitate to report bullying, exclusion, or emotional distress directly to teachers or parents due to fear or embarrassment. Schools struggle to monitor emotional wellbeing across multiple classrooms, often leading to delayed intervention in serious situations.

The Solution
Tammy AI provides a safe conversational interface where students interact using voice input. The AI listens, analyzes emotional urgency in real time, and classifies conversations into GREEN, YELLOW, or RED severity levels.

Normal conversations remain private, while serious concerns trigger counselor awareness alerts, enabling timely and responsible intervention.
